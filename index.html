About Us

With over a decade of experience in the logistics industry, we have become a trusted partner

Committed to Creating More Efficient and Intelligent Connections

Established in 2012, AIRESPACE EXPRESS CONTAINER has evolved from a regional transportation company to an integrated logistics service provider with a national network and cross-border capabilities. We always prioritize customer needs.

The company owns over 500 self-operated vehicles, collaborates with more than 2,000 additional vehicles, and manages warehouse space exceeding 300,000 square meters. We continuously invest in technology, with our self-developed "Changfeng" logistics management system enabling end-to-end visibility and intelligent management from order placement to delivery.
